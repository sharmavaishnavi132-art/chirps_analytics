Our Project Mission

The Voice of Nature

Bridging AI and Conservation

Chirps Analytics is a bio-acoustic research platform. We use Artificial Intelligence to analyze bird vocalizations, turning raw audio recordings into meaningful data for biodiversity monitoring.

The Vision

To provide a non-invasive, data-driven approach to wildlife preservation. We believe that by understanding the "acoustic fingerprints" of our forests, we can better protect the species that live within them.

Why Birds?

Birds are key indicators of ecosystem health. Changes in their population or migration patterns often signal broader environmental shifts.

How It Works

Our Deep Learning models identify species from audio files uploaded by users, allowing for rapid and accurate field research without disturbing the habitat.
